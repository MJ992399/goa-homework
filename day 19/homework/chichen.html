Chichén Itzá

Situated in Yucatan, Mexico, Chichén Itzá was a city built by the Mayan people.During the spring and fall equinoxes, a shadow forms on the temple’s north stairway, creating the illusion of a slithering serpent. Today, 26 ruins still stand in Chichén Itzá, and approximately 2.5 million people visit the site every year.
